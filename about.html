Searching for ruin
Neri & Hu Design and Research, founded by partners Lyndon Neri and Rosanna Hu, is a highly acclaimed and much sought after interdisciplinary architecture and design practice based in Shanghai, China. The acclaimed international firm is known for creating captivating total environments, including architecture and master planning, interiors, graphic, and product design services. Neri and Hu furniture are well known pieces that exemplify the creativity they exude. Beyond their critically lauded environments, the firm is actively engaged in industrial and product design for many prestigious European manufacturers in addition to their own eponymous “neri&hu” product line.
Process is constant

Every Neri & Hu desgin project begins with extensive design research, a process that anchors the work in the dynamic and complex interaction of experience, detail, material, form, and light as opposed to conforming to a formulaic style.

Neri & Hu’s global purview is the result of the founders’ education and professional trajectories. Mr. Neri received a Bachelor of Architecture at the University of California at Berkeley and a Master of Architecture at Harvard University. Prior to starting Neri & Hu, he was the Director for Projects in Asia and an Associate for Michael Graves & Associates in Princeton for over 10 years. Neri also worked in New York City for various blue chip architectural firms.

Dissolve and grow

Neri and Hu are also the founders of Design Republic, a retail concept store in Shanghai offering unique products by the world's best design talents, many of which are not otherwise available to consumers in China. The flagship store design received the Perspective Awards Best Interior Retail and the Design For Asia Best Design of Greater China.

The firm’s core vision is a global worldview incorporating overlapping design disciplines to create a new paradigm in architecture and design. It has a multi-cultural staff that speaks over 30 different languages and is currently working on projects in countries all over the globe.
